Key Insights

These publications demonstrate early genomic discoveries in uveitis, including:

  • Identification of disease-associated loci in specific uveitis subtypes (birdshot, anterior)
  • Shared genetic pathways between uveitis and systemic inflammatory diseases
  • Role of complement and coagulation pathways in immune-mediated uveitis
  • Growing evidence for the value of international genomic collaboration
